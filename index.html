MARKET FOCUS

Education Sector

Our primary focus is on university students in engineering fields such as mechatronics, robotics, automation, and industrial engineering. These students often require support in the development of academic projects, prototypes, and practical implementations of theoretical concepts.

We provide accessible and functional solutions that allow students to better understand real industrial systems, helping them bridge the gap between theory and real-world applications through hands-on experience.

Developers

We also support independent developers and university teams who are working on innovation projects, prototypes, or technological solutions.

Our services help transform ideas into functional systems by providing guidance, design support, and technical implementation for academic and semi-professional environments.

Industrial Companies

Although our main focus is academic, we also aim to collaborate with companies seeking automation solutions to improve efficiency and reduce costs.

We offer scalable systems that can be adapted from educational prototypes to industrial-level implementations.
